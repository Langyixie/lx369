    Immersive audio has been an obvious and irreversible trend in the audio industry as the VR set becomes more popular and the price becomes cheaper. Different from playing on traditional single or duo loudspeakers, immersive audio often requires stricter playback environment: including VR headset, surround system sparkers or earphones, and position tracker. Thus, listener can have a much better experience to waer a headphone while listening to the 3D audio.
